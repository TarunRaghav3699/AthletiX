What is AthletiX?

AthletiX is a college sports management platform built to empower student-athletes, coaches, and campus sports organizers. Our mission is to make athlete tracking, event management, and community engagement seamless and data-driven. With features like athlete performance tracking, organized event registration, live score updates, leaderboards, and social feeds, AthletiX helps institutions create a safer, more competitive, and more connected sports ecosystem.

Athlete Tracking
Detailed stats, progress charts, and training logs.
Event Management
Create fixtures, accept registrations, and manage results.
Community
Announcements, social integrations, and campus leaderboards.
